- Products: IPL hair removal devices, LED/DPL beauty devices
- All IPL models use xenon quartz lamp tube
- All IPL models wavelength: **530–1200nm** (broader than Fansizhe's standard 560nm models)

**Note on Semlamp-Fansizhe relationship:** K253881 (FDA) includes model SL-B505WM as a Semlamp model cleared under Fansizhe's FDA submission. ## Notes on Semlamp's "Age Spot" Filter Heads

The SL-B328-2, SL-B301-2, SL-B300-2, SL-B330-1, and SL-B287-1 come with an "Age Spot Remove Head" (amber/yellow filter visible in product photos) and "Acne Remove Head" (orange-red filter).

**What these heads likely do:**
- The amber "age spot" head likely acts as a short-pass or bandpass filter, concentrating the output into a shorter wavelength range (possibly 530–700nm) that better targets epidermal melanin
- The orange "acne" head may target the 415nm or blue-light range for P. acnes photoinactivation — though at IPL energy levels this is secondary
- These are the closest thing in the home market to the clinical practice of switching to a 515nm filter for pigment work vs a 560nm filter for hair

**Caveat:** The filter heads don't increase the device's total energy output — they just redistribute the spectrum. So if anything, they reduce total fluence by cutting out wavelengths the lamp emits. The effective J/cm² on target may be lower than the base spec.
