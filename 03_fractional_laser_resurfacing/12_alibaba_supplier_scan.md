# Alibaba Supplier Scan (live browse)

**Method:** browsed Alibaba.com live via the Chrome extension on **2026-06-21**, searching "1450nm fractional laser home," "1927nm fractional laser home use," "Focuslight 1470nm laser module," and the Suppliers tab for "unimed laser." Prices are as-listed (USD), MOQ-dependent, and change constantly. This confirms/extends [04_suppliers_and_companies](04_suppliers_and_companies.md) and [10_where_to_buy_storefronts](10_where_to_buy_storefronts.md).

---

## 1. Headline findings

1. **There is NO consumer *handheld* 1450/1927nm fractional device on Alibaba** — nothing in the YDUNVIE Iris / MimiSilk / Tria FRX class. Every "fractional" result is a **clinic/salon machine** ($890–$42,000), an **OEM laser module/source**, or an **IPL hair-removal handheld**. This directly confirms md04's call: the home fractional handhelds are **brand-controlled, not open Alibaba SKUs.**
2. **Jiangsu Unimed / YDUNVIE has NO findable Alibaba storefront.** A Suppliers-tab search for "unimed laser" returned only unrelated firms (Hebei JT Medical; "Suzhou Unimes" = POS terminals). → To buy the Iris/Dora you still go YDUNVIE/MimiSilk direct (md10), not Alibaba.
3. **Focuslight has NO Alibaba storefront either** — consistent with it being a large public B2B company that sells via its own sales team. But Alibaba has **many alternative 1470nm/1440nm laser-module suppliers** if you want to commission a build (see §4).
4. **"Home use" on Alibaba is marketing.** Several $6k–$12k standing/portable *clinic* machines are titled "for Home Use" (Beijing Superlaser, PZ Laser). They are not consumer devices.
5. **Most "1470nm" hits are endolaser/lipolysis** (980+1470nm fat-dissolving/vein), **not** fractional skin resurfacing. Only Gigaalaser and Shandong Huamei sell 1470nm *fractional skin* units. Don't confuse the two.

> Takeaway: the **laser engine is a commodity on Alibaba** (you can even buy a 1440nm diode chip). What YDUNVIE/Tria actually sell is the **integration** — scanner, cooling, eye-safety, firmware, enclosure, regulatory clearance — which is *not* a stock Alibaba item. That's the moat, and why there's no drop-in clone.

## 6. Bottom line (answering "find anything similar / who makes md4–md10 devices")

- **Similar finished product to buy on Alibaba?** No true match. Closest *technology* = Gigaalaser / Huamei **1470nm fractional** clinic units ($890–4,000), and the **1927nm thulium** clinic machines ($6.5k–12k) from Superlaser / PZ Laser / Keylaser / Huamei — but all are **clinic/desktop, not the handheld** you researched.
- **Who likely makes the md4/md10 devices?** The **YDUNVIE/Iris/Dora family maker (Jiangsu Unimed) is NOT on Alibaba** — it's a closed brand. The **clinic-machine suppliers in md04 ARE on Alibaba** (table §2). The **OEM laser modules** behind any of them are commodity (§4).
- **Action if buying a finished home device:** stick with md10's channels (YDUNVIE direct / MimiSilk / myernk / Jofiou) — Alibaba won't get you the Iris or Dora.
- **Action if exploring a clinic machine or a custom build:** Alibaba is the right place — start with **Gigaalaser (1470nm NAF)** or a **1927nm thulium** unit from Superlaser/PZ/Keylaser, and **Jlight/Shenzhen Super Laser** for modules.
