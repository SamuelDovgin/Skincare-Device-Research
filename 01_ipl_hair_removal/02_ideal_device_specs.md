## Why Each Spec Matters

### Filter Cutoff ≤530nm
Clinical photorejuvenation uses 515–570nm filters. The 510–530nm range captures:
- The secondary HbO₂ absorption peak at 542nm (within band)
- The tertiary peak at 577nm (within band)  
- Maximum melanin absorption (shorter = stronger for melanin)

A 560nm cutoff clips the 542nm HbO₂ peak entirely. For redness treatment, 50nm of filter difference is significant. All of Fansizhe's 510nm models (T023A, T021A, T021K, T015K, T015C, T026, T035K, T016K, T058, T036K, T012, T011C, T013C, T019) and all Semlamp models (530nm) are acceptable. Semlamp's 530nm is a reasonable compromise.

### Fluence ≥8 J/cm²
Minimum clinical threshold for any vascular response is ~15 J/cm² — home devices cannot reach this. But within the home device universe, higher fluence is directionally better. The difference between 4 J/cm² and 8 J/cm² matters:
- 4 J/cm²: sub-photobiomodulation threshold for most targets; primarily SHR hair-removal territory
- 6–8 J/cm²: produces measurable thermal response in superficial melanin; marginal for vascular; adequate for hair
- >8 J/cm²: approaches the low end of pigment-clearing fluence in fair skin

### Large Spot Size (3–4cm²)
Larger spot = deeper effective penetration (beam scattering less problematic at large aperture). Also faster coverage for full-face treatment. Face-only is typically done with 3cm² head.

### Contact Cooling
Essential for higher energy levels. Allows pushing to level 4–5 safely. Sapphire crystal maintains lower temp at contact vs passive metal/gel panel.

### Single + Dual Pulse
For vascular work: want controllable single pulse or dual pulse with ≤30ms gap, NOT SHR burst mode. Ideally device has both modes and you can select.

### FDA Clearance
FDA clearance (OHT) requires the manufacturer to prove the device actually produces the rated energy under testing. Uncertified devices on Alibaba commonly misrepresent actual output. NMPA (China) is also credible. CE mark is minimal in terms of energy verification.

## Red Flags to Avoid on Alibaba

- Energy spec listed only as total across triple pulse with no single-pulse spec
- Mode listed only as "Dual" or SHR with no explicit single-pulse spec
- No mention of cooling
- Wavelength listed as only "560–1200nm" or "640–1200nm" — skip for your goals
- No FDA/NMPA/CE certification mentioned
- Suspiciously low price (<$20) — almost certainly fake specs
- Advertised as 100,000+ flashes with no energy spec — flash count means nothing
