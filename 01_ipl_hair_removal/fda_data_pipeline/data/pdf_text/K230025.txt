Discussion 2
There are some differences on the energy density and laser beam between
proposed device and predicate device. The laser beam of the proposed
device is larger than that of the predicate device. However, the energy
density is lower of the proposed device, and both of the devices comply
with IEC 60825-1. Therefore, the safety and effectiveness won’t be
affected and they can be considered substantially equivalent in safety and
effectiveness.
Discussion 3

they can be considered substantially equivalent in safety and effectiveness.
