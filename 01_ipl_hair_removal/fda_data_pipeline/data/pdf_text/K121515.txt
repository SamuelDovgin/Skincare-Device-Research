4. Device Description
The lsolaz® 2 System delivers non-coherent 400-1200 mm and 550-1200 run
intense pulsed light via a delivery handpiecc utilizing photopneumatic
technology. The system consists of a main console and a treatment handpiece.
The desired power and delivery time are set by the operator. The modifications
made to the device were to reduce and simplify~ device settings.
5. Intended Use:
The Isolaz® 2 System is intended for treatment of mild to moderate acne,
including pustular acne, comedonal acne and mild to moderate inflammatory
acne (acne vulgaris) in all skin types (Fitzpatrick [-VI).
