Indications for Use (Describe)

The SmoothSkin Pure Switch is intended for the removal of unwanted hair. The SmoothSkin Pure
Switch is also indicated for the permanent reduction in hair regrowth, defined as the long-term,
stable reduction in the number of hairs regrowing when measured at 6, 9 and 12 months after the
completion of a treatment regime.
