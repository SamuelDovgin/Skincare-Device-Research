IV. Device Description
IPL Hair Removal Device, is an over-the-counter, home-use device for unwanted hair reduction by using Intense Pulsed
Light (IPL), and it has been designed 3 models with the same IPL technology for hair removal, which is model JM-843,JM865,JM-638. The device works below the skin’s surface and does not involve any cutting or pulling, reducing hair growth
Page 2 of 8

SHENZHEN JUNMEI TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD.

with minimal pain.
The device is only powered by the external power adapter and its IPL emission activation is by finger switch.
IPL Hair Removal Device is a Pulsed Light system which emits intense pulsed light (IPL) at a wavelength ranging from 5301200nm and 500-1200nm. The device works on the principles of selective photothermolysis. That is, causing thermal
damage to target chromophores by using light of appropriate wavelength in pulses that exceeds the chromophores ’
thermal relaxation time but sparing normal skin by limiting the pulse width below the thermal relaxation time for skin.
