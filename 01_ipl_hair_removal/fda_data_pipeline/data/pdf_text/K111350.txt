aesthetic and cosmetic applications requiring selective
photothermolysis (photocoagulation or coagulation) and hemostasis
of soft tissue.

> Removal of unwanted hair from all skin types (640nm to
l200nm);
> Treatment of benign pigmented lesions including
dlyschromia, hyper pigmentation, melasma, ephelides

veins(485 nm to l200nm); and
)> Treatment of benign cutaneous lesions including warts,
scars and striae (420nm to l200nm).
The Medicam Evolase pulsed light system (and included
