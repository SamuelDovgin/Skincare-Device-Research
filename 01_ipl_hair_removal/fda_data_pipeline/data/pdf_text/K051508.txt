4. Device Description
The MED flash 11 Intense Pulsed Light System is a medical device emitting light
radiation in the range from 590 nm to 1200 rnm. The system is based on a quick
power discharge of capacitors in a Xenon lamp, mounted on a handpiece. This
generates a rapid and Intense Pulsed Light.
