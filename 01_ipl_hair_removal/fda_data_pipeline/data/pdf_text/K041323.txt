Description of the New Star
PRIMA Pulsed Light Therapy
System:

The CoolTouch PRIMA Pulsed Light Therapy System is a
compact, self-contained system that delivers a beam of pulsed
light at wavelengths of 300nm to 1400nm, which can be
optimized at various wavelength ranges and delivered to the
treatment site. The system consists of a control console unit,
which houses the power supply, cooling system, cryogen
source, and microcontroller, the handpiece, which contains the
light source, and the footswitch.

Intended use of the New Star
PRIMA Pulsed Light Therapy
System:

The CoolTouch PRIMA Pulsed Light Therapy System is
indicated for the treatment of psoriasis, vitiligo, atopic
dermatitis (eczema), seborrheic dermatitis, vascular lesions,
rosacea, hemangiomas, leg veins, hair removal, tattoos,
pigmented lesions, pigmented lesions, lentigenes, and mild to

moderate inflammatory acne vulgaris.
