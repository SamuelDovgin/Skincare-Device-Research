IV Device description
The IPL Treatment Systems utilizes an intense, visible, broad-spectrum pulse of light
that when used with interchangeable filters, allowing it to selectively heat the target

for different indications. The optical component is intended to drive the IPL energy to
concentrate where the optical energy has selectively heated the target.
The Intense pulsed light (IPL) is a type of intensive, broadband, coherent light source
which has a wavelength spectrum of 420 nm -1200 nm. The light-based component
delivers optical energy that is absorbed by specific chromophores in the skin, which
is converted to heat, according to the principle of selective photothermolysis.
The proposed device consists of the main unit, control panel and the treatment
handpiece(s).
