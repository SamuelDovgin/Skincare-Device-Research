DEVICE DESCRIPTION
The sensiLight Mini device is a pulsed light hair removal device. Light-based hair
removal is based on the theory of selective photothermolysis in which optical
energy is used to disable hair growth. The sensiLight Mini device is composed of
a hand held applicator and an external power supply. The spot size (treatment
area) in the sensiLight Mini device is 3 cm2.
The device contains a lamp, a skin proximity sensor and a skin pigmentation
sensor to detect appropriate skin tones. If the sensiLight Mini is not properly
applied (in full contact with the skin) or user skin tone is too dark/tanned, the
sensiLight Mini will not trigger a pulse.

5.

INTENDED USE/ INDICATIONS FOR USE
The sensiLight Mini is an over the counter device intended for the removal of
unwanted hair. The sensiLight Mini is also intended for permanent reduction in
hair regrowth, defined as a long-term, stable reduction in the number of hairs regrowing when measured at 6, 9, and 12 months after the completion of treatment
regimen.
