F. Description
iPulseLight IPL System is a type of intensive, broadband, coherent light source which
has a wavelength spectrum of 420 nm -1200 nm. With these special properties, the IPL
has a wide application in non-ablative therapies based on theory of human skin tissue's
selective absorption and photothermolysis of light sources. Meanwhile, IPL treatment is
more effective, with no downtime and can make the patients get recovered more quickly
than conventional therapies.
