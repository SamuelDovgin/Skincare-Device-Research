4. Device description
The Light Based Hair Removal Device is a personal, light-based, hair reduction
device intended to be sold over-the counter directly to the end user. The device
provides hair reduction using IPL technology. The device consists of IPL main
body and adapter two parts, and a lamp cap located in the main body which is the
source of optical radiation, namely a Xenon flashlamp. Meanwhile, the device is
powered from adapter via an external power.
5. Intended use
The Light based hair removal device is an over-the-counter device intended for
removal of unwanted hair such as but not limited to small areas such as underarm
