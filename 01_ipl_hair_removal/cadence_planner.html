Why spacing beats frequency — the hair cycle

IPL only destroys a follicle in its anagen (growth) phase. Only part of your hair is anagen at once, so you wait for the next cohort — treating daily can't beat this ceiling.

Per-session ceiling ≈ 10–25% (the anagen fraction present that day) — not raised by treating sooner. [AAD] [StatPearls]

Safety guardrails

Never treat the same area more than once a week. Beyond weekly is wasted (no new anagen target) and stacks burn/pigment risk. Shave — don't wax/pluck — between sessions. No tanning or sun before/after; treat only untanned skin. Home IPL works best on dark hair + light–medium skin and isn't validated for the darkest tones (check your skin-tone chart). Stop & see a dermatologist for blistering, crusting, or new dark/light patches.
